Doner

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef in large pieces
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 teaspoon plain yogurt 1 teaspoon tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon of vinegar (of any kind) 1/2 teaspoon of thyme Salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 heaping tablespoon of butter

Steps

  1. Grate the onion Add yoghurt, tomato paste, vinegar, thyme, salt and olive oil and mix. Add the beef into it and mix until all pieces are covered in sauce
  2. Take the pieces over a large piece of plastic wrap and wrap it into a thick strip. Set aside in the freezer for 5-6 hours
  3. Remove the meat from the freezer and chop it very very thin with a large, sharp knife. Melt the butter in a large pan and take the meat slices into the pan and stir fry in high heat until partly brownish
